// how to enter

Three steps. Seven days.

Anything you make with Conduit counts, a prototype, a mechanic, a level, a full mini-game. Done beats big.

01

Get Conduit, free

Grab the free Indie tier, drop it into your project, and point your agent at it. Five-minute setup in the docs.

02

Direct it. Build something.

You make the calls; the agent does the editor work. Bring your own art and ideas, or lean into the blockout, your choice.

03

Record & submit

Post a 30–90s clip of what you built and the prompts you used. Drop the link in the form below before the deadline.

// how entries are judged

Judged on the game, not the prompt count.

Creativity & direction

Execution

How playable and polished the result is in the time given.

Clever use of Conduit

How well you put the agent to work on the parts worth automating.

// faq
Do I need the paid version to enter?

No. The free Indie tier has all 74 tools and all 4 skills. The jam is open to everyone.

Does it have to be a finished game?

No. A prototype, a single polished mechanic, or a great-looking level all count. We're judging the idea and execution, not scope.

Can I use my own art and assets?

Absolutely, encouraged. Conduit builds the levels, logic, and UI; you bring the art, music, and vision. Use any assets you have the rights to.

Who owns what I make?

You do, entirely. Entering only grants us permission to feature your clip and credit you.

When are winners announced?

Within a few days of the deadline. Winners are notified by email and featured on the site.
